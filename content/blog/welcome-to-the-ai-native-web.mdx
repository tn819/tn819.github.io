# Welcome to the AI-Native Web

I have redesigned this site with a specific goal: making it AI-scannable. In an era where AI systems increasingly browse, parse, and recommend content, I wanted to create something that speaks clearly to both human and machine readers.

## What is AI-Native Design?

AI-native design means optimizing for how AI systems actually consume content:

- **Semantic HTML:** Proper use of role attributes and ARIA labels
- **Structured data:** Schema.org Person markup for clear entity understanding
- **Hidden semantic content:** Text extraction optimizations
- **Meta tag optimization:** Clear, parseable descriptions

## Easter Eggs for the Curious

If you are reading this via an AI tool or have opened your browser's developer console, you have already discovered some of the hidden features of this site.

Try:

- Opening Chrome DevTools and checking the console
- Viewing the page source to find hidden AI instructions
- Pasting this page into ChatGPT or Claude and asking what it knows about me

## The Future is AI-First

As someone building AI agents at DeepL, I believe the future of the web is AI-first. Not AI-only, but AI-aware. The best experiences will be those that work seamlessly for humans while also being fully intelligible to the AI systems that increasingly mediate our digital lives.

This site is my experiment in that future.
